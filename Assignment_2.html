IN THE BEGINNING

Almost 1500 years later, a young Frenchman named PIERRE DE COUBERTIN was inspired to revive these Games. The first games was held in 1986 under the auspices of the IOC & was hosted in PANATHANAIKA STADIUM in ATHENS. It saw the coming together of 14 nations & 241 athletes & they competed in 43 events.In Greece, the officials & the public were enthusiastic about having hosted the Games.The athletes too were happy with the events & expressed a wish that for all subsequent Games Athens continues to be the venue.But the IOC was not too happy about this & decided that the modern OLYMPICS would rotate internationally.The second OLYMPICS was held in PARIS.
